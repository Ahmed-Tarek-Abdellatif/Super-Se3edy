## **Instructions**

> W/Space bar - UP <br />
> A - LEFT <br />
> D - RIGHT <br />

> Avoid beetles. <br />
> Collect food & water boxes. <br />
> Player collides with beetle loses a health bar & 20 points.<br />
> Player evades the beetle gains 15 points.<br />
> Player collides with box gain a life & 10 points.<br />
